# 🌾 FarmTwin AI - Digital Twin & Smart Farm Intelligence Platform

## 📌 Overview

FarmTwin AI is an AI-powered smart agriculture web application that creates a digital representation of a farmer's field and provides intelligent farming assistance.

The platform helps farmers monitor crop health, predict yield, optimize irrigation, manage fertilizer usage, and identify possible farming risks using AI-driven insights.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🌱 Smart Farm Digital Twin
- Create digital farm profiles
- Store crop information
- Manage soil details
- Track farming activities

### 🤖 AI Crop Health Monitoring
- Detect plant diseases from crop images
- Identify nutrient deficiencies
- Provide recovery suggestions

### 💧 Smart Irrigation & Fertilizer Advisor
- Recommend watering schedules
- Suggest fertilizer requirements
- Reduce resource wastage

### 📊 Yield & Profit Prediction
- Predict expected crop production
- Estimate farming income
- Analyze crop performance

### ⚠️ Farm Risk Alert System
- Pest attack alerts
- Weather risk notifications
- Crop failure risk prediction
